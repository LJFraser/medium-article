How many countries in the world are there?

There are 195 countries in the world today. This total comprises 193 countries that are member states of the United Nations and 2 countries that are non-member observer states: the Holy See and the State of Palestine.

Which are the most difficult to visit?

The hardest countries to travel to are usually the ones who are experiencing political instability and wars, which makes security a real concern. Afghanistan and Iraq, for example, are high on this list because of what they are going through at the moment. Other countries that I have been to that are definitely out of bounds for most travelers include South Sudan, Somalia, the Congo DRC or North Korea. Venezuela is also a No GO zone right now.

Which are the easiest to visit?

It’s not a secret that the easiest countries to travel should be around North America and Western Europe. This is usually a trip where most people starting to travel can do and where you dont really need to be a long-term dedicated traveler to venture in a week or 2 weeks holiday. With little to no experience, one can easily get around most of these countries. I myself started with the usual trips in the US when I was around 14 and then my first trip to Europe at 17.
